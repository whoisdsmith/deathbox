## Installation (For README.md)

To add any of these banners to your GitHub README:

1. Copy the banner of your choice
2. Paste it at the top of your README.md inside triple backticks:

```
# Your existing title and content goes here
```

3. For best display, ensure your README has no content above the banner

Recommended: Option 1 or Option 4 work best on GitHub's default width settings.
